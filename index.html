Vision

The MAGIS party aims to make the students of the Junior High School enjoy the next new school year with the changes it wishes to implement. The party leans towards a CHANGE for the students, such as those within school facilities (e.g. CR improvement), connectivity, campus comfortability, openness, health (e.g. mental health, physical health), transition, and more. Overall, we aim to create platforms and activities that would satisfy the needs of the students.
